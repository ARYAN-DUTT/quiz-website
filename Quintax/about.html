About

Welcome to Quintax, your go-to platform for testing your skills in Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, our interactive quizzes will help you sharpen your coding abilities and track your progress.

Before starting your quiz, you will be provided with a unique username and password by our team to access the platform. You can choose whether to take the Python test or focus on front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, based on your interests and learning goals. Your scores will be displayed at the end of the test.
